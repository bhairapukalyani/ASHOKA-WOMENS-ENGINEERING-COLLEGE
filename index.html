About Our College

Our History

Founded in 2008, Ashoka Women's Engineering College has been a pioneer in women's technical education in the region. From humble beginnings with just two departments, we've grown to become a center of excellence with state-of-the-art facilities.

Our Mission

Think Ashoka Think Placements.To empower women through quality technical education, fostering innovation, leadership, and social responsibility while bridging the gender gap in STEM fields.
